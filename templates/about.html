About The Village Bookshelf

The Village Bookshelf is a platform for sharing books within local communities.

We promote literacy and sustainability by making it easy to swap books with your neighbours. You might finally find that one book you’ve been searching for!

How It Works

New to the site? Start by searching for books using the search bar or browsing the scrolling list on the homepage.

To request a book swap, you’ll need to sign up and log in. You can sign up from the Home, About, or Login pages.

Once logged in, click on any book (not added by you) to view its detail page and request a swap.

You can also add your own books to the library from the Book List page. Just click “Add Book,” fill out the form, and your listing will be live.

When someone requests your book, you’ll receive an email notification.

Happy book swapping!
